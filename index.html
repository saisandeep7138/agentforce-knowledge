NimbusTech Solutions - Product Knowledge Base

NimbusTech Solutions is a cloud software provider offering business applications for sales, marketing, analytics, billing, and customer support teams. All products are subscription-based and hosted in the cloud.

Product 1: NimbusCRM

Overview

NimbusCRM is a customer relationship management system that helps companies manage leads, contacts, and sales opportunities.

Key Features

  • Lead tracking
  • Opportunity pipeline management
  • Email integration
  • Task reminders
  • Mobile access

Pricing

Starter: $25 per user per month

Professional: $55 per user per month

Enterprise: $95 per user per month

Best Use Case

Best suited for sales teams and account managers.

Product 2: NimbusDesk

Overview

NimbusDesk is a helpdesk ticketing and customer support platform.

Key Features

  • Ticket management system
  • Live chat support
  • Knowledge base portal
  • SLA tracking
  • Customer portal

Pricing

Basic: $20 per agent per month

Growth: $45 per agent per month

Enterprise: $80 per agent per month

Best Use Case

Ideal for customer service teams and support centers.

Product 3: NimbusAnalytics

Overview

NimbusAnalytics is a reporting and dashboard platform that provides business insights.

Key Features

  • Interactive dashboards
  • Custom reports
  • Forecasting tools
  • Data visualization charts
  • Export reports to Excel and PDF

Pricing

Standard: $40 per user per month

Advanced: $75 per user per month

Best Use Case

Used by managers and analysts who need performance tracking and reporting.

  • Lead scoring
  • Landing page builder
  • Marketing automation workflows
  • Campaign performance analytics

Pricing

Growth: $70 per month (10,000 contacts)

Pro: $120 per month (50,000 contacts)
